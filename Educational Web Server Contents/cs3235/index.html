Project Introduction

The rise of personal computing and the Internet of Things (IoT) devices, we are now, more than ever, susceptible to threats over the Internet. The pervasiveness of cyber-related attacks and exploits are very much tied to our interconnectedness via the devices that we use, therefore the aim of this project is to raise awareness on the various risks that we unknowingly face, as well as how they work.


With respect to growing concern in regards to the threat of insecure networks, the team has developed this website as an educational tool to demonstrate theories and proof of concepts with regards to the dangers of insecure networks through first-hand experience. In addition to providing insight into how the attacks (DNS spoofing, network sniffing and session cookie stealing) work through giving rundowns of the processes that take place when a system/device is getting compromised, we have also come up with a possible security solution that could identify and counter these vulnerabilities.
